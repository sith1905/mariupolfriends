Helihool

We make high-quality life music accessible for patients in hospitals and care homes.

What?

We are a Nonprofit Organization, Charity organisation.

Where?

Based in Tallinn, Estonia.

Why?

We make high-quality life music accessible for patients in hospitals and care homes.

We need musicians!

JOIN US

How?

Our performers play music and share their hearts and souls with people in each care establishment. By doing so, we establish long-lasting friendship and trust, and sometimes even hope with patients in healthcare facilities. Concert repertoire is chosen carefully, wide range of people's musical tastes and preferences always taken into account.

We know that communication matters! That is why each performance has a beforehand chat with the audience and afterwards tea party. During concerts, audience members are encouraged to ask questions and participate as much as they want and feel comfortable.

Helihool provides an enjoyable social experience for people in care, the staff and visiting families and friends.

Benefits?

We offer hospitals managers an instrument of live-music experience to improve their environment health service quality and reputation.
